Design

The layout for this project was not modified much from the initial layout plans. It is a simple design that is logically laid out. By that I mean, the game title is at the top with the game music placed just under it and outside of the "game container". Then, inside the container, you have the directions at the top followed by the enemy score (which is the score you need to know before you start the game. After that, the sailor scorce, sailor buttons, and win/lose container are placed.

The color scheme was based off the colors used from the backgrounds of the anime, as well as the scouts' colors.
