Website Concept

Hockey Fanatics is a group project we came up with during my coding boot camp days. The main concept is that the website gives the user an insite into a team's chance of winning based on previous winnings. Overall, the website features account signup/login - in order to save a favorite team(s), team rosters, and team schedules, which provide date of the match, opponent, time, and percentage of winning. A few future endeavoures we had for the website were a forum, partnership with shops that legally sell official NHL Merchandise, and partner with ticket sellers.
